Decanting Conscious Awareness and Neural Timing

DCANT Lab is a research lab housed at Sogang University in Seoul, South Korea. We study visual perception and awareness, focusing on topics such as binocular rivalry, perceptual rhythms, and ensemble perception. Our research seeks to understand the influence of perceptual rhythms in visual processing and their interactions with visual awareness. We also investigate aspects of vision where awareness extends beyond visual processing capacities—for example, how we read the atmosphere of a crowd even when unable to process each face individually (ensemble perception).
